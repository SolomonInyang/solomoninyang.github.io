Language Bot

An educational game for learning French. The player gains points by helping
a robot character translate procedurally generated French phrases to English.
One life is lost for every word the player gets incorrect and the game ends when
the player loses all three of their lives.

Occult Island

An experimental simulation horror game developed as part of my
honours thesis at the Australian National University. The player assigns
tasks to a group of survivors that have been shipwrecked on an island in
order to help them escape. The game introduces a novel game AI technique called
"Mental State Modelling" which is used to create believable autonomous agents
that exhibit behaviours based on various internal mental states including
perceptions, motivations and emotions.

Culture Crafter

A mythology simulation game where you create your own
culture hero who goes on mythical adventures to gain the power
to shape the culture of a society.
